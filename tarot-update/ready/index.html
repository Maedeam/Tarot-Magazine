Welcome to the Tarot Magazine

The Tarot Magazine is one the fiercely independent and proudly subversive and gutsy voice in today’s media landscape, dedicated to publishing smart, progressive commentary on amazing world of tarot from mostly social perspective. We’re thrilled to changed the public view of tarot as a magical instrument to a source which is offering a wealth of inspiration and subject matter and has a connection to the world of art as well.

Issue Two
Tarot & CULTURE

Many theories exist regarding the cultural origins of Tarot cards and it’s difficult to separate historical facts from occult fiction. The obvious truth, though, is that the images and symbols displayed on the cards were common in Medieval and Early Modern literature, art, and theater. this issue contains article mainly concentrate on studying tsrot from cultural perspectives
